In 1978, we opened our doors and our hearts to the community and everyone gave their love back. Being a fine dining restaurant, A-LA-AISH or better known as al-haish has something for everyone. It is an upscale place, a meeting place, a place where you can come and experience a unique Culinary experience right here in Malibu. The family behind A-LA-AISH have been innovators and pioneers in the hospitality industry for over 15 years. With the opening of A-LA-AISH, we are committed to enhancing our patrons' experience by providing a Fine Dining Experience. Our extensive menu made with local, fresh ingredients and our world class desserts will leave your palate yearning for more.
